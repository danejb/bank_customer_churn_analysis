## ðŸ’¡ Top Recommendations

### Priority 1: Fix Year 0-1 Onboarding (HIGHEST IMPACT)
- *Current:* 23% churn
- *Target:* 18% (5% improvement)
- *Benefit:* Retain 50+ additional customers per 1,000 cohort
- *Action:* Redesign onboarding, simplify first year, provide support

### Priority 2: Replicate Year 4-7 Success
- *Finding:* Year 4-7 show lowest churn (17.22%)
- *Action:* Study what works, apply to year 0-1
- *Benefit:* Accelerate new customers to stable phase

### Priority 3: Protect Long-Term Customers
- *Finding:* Year 9+ churn at 21.75% and rising
- *Action:* Create loyalty programs, re-engagement campaigns
- *Benefit:* Preserve high-value relationships

### Priority 4: Dempgraphic Segmentation
- *Finding:* Age groups have different churn patterns
- *Action:* Develop age-specific retention strategies
- *Benefit:* Higher retention with targeted approach
